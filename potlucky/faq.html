FAQ

How far in advance do I need to order?

We need at least one day's notice in order to have time to share and prepare.

What is your cancellation policy?

You must cancel two days ahead of time otherwise there is no refund.

Do I need to return anything?

We will deliver your food using disposable containers unless you provide your own. If your item requires a serving spoon or other utensil, you must provide it. There is no need to return anything.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, Paypal and Venmo. Sorry, but we do not accept personal checks.
