Typography

Heading Font: Josefin Sans

I wanted to use a sans-serif font because it feels more welcoming than other font styles. This particular font was a bit distinctive.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans

This sans-serif font is easy to read on the web and plays well with Josefin Sans. Even though they are the same style, they differ enough.
