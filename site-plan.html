Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote the hotel chain by letting the target audience know what specialized amenities we have to offer so they will want to stay with us and make reservations.

Audience

My audience is members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who are temple workers or patrons and their families

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

I chose this sans serif font because it works with Open Sans well and suggests a friendly tone.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans

I chose this font because of it's readability. This sans-serif font works in paragraphs and it is also very friendly looking.
